# Terms of Service (ToS)
**Last Updated: April 2026**

Welcome to **SCS (Shazly Community for Students)**. By registering, accessing, or using our platform, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms.

## 1. Account Roles & Responsibilities
When registering, users must select an accurate role:
- **Student:** Access to libraries, courses, groups, and purchasing rights.
- **Teacher:** Ability to manage students, host online meetings, and moderate classrooms.
- **Creator (Platform Owner/Author):** Ability to publish books, upload courses, and create bespoke platforms inside SCS.
- **Normal User:** Limited access to basic social features and preview content until a specific role is designated.

*SCS reserves the right to ask for identity verification for Teachers and Creators to protect the integrity of the community.*

## 2. Content Ownership and Licensing
- Users retain copyright over the original material (videos, books, documents) they upload to SCS.
- By uploading content, Creators grant SCS a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to host, display, and distribute this content through the platform.

## 3. Revenue Sharing and Financial Transactions
All transactions are processed via secure gateways (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Fawry, InstaPay). 
When selling content or subscriptions on the platform, revenue is split according to the following matrix:
- **Books:** SCS retains 30% of the sale price.
- **Individual Courses:** SCS retains 20% of the sale price.
- **Nested Platforms (Creators running their own academies):** SCS retains 15% of the total ecosystem revenue routed through that platform.

Creators may request withdrawal of their available funds (Wallet Balance) via the Admin Dashboard. SCS reserves the right to withhold funds pending investigation into fraudulent or abnormal transaction activity via our automated Fraud Detection System.

## 4. Subscriptions and Fair Use
SCS offers Subscription Tiers:
- **Free:** Ad-supported, standard UI tools, strict rate-limiting on AI features.
- **Silver:** Ad-free experience, increased quota for AI interactions and tools.
- **Gold:** Unrestricted platform experience, unlimited AI usage and tooling within acceptable broad usage constraints.

SCS reserves the right to terminate or pause subscriptions if abusive bot-like behavior or extreme network utilization is detected.

## 5. Community Guidelines & Smart Moderation
The community thrives on respect and learning.
- **Hate Speech & Harassment:** Strictly prohibited.
- **NSFW/Graphic Content:** Not allowed.
- **Piracy:** Distributing copyrighted material without authorization is an offense. SCS utilizes DRM measures and Screenshot-Detection tools; bypassing these tools violates this ToS.
- **Smart Moderation:** SCS utilizes an AI-based smart moderation engine. If a user is reported, the AI system compiles a complete historical audit (including deleted messages) for Admin review.

## 6. Access and Termination
The SCS Administration retains total control over the platform. The Admin Dashboard grants authority to ban users, delete courses, and remove nested LMS platforms if they violate the ToS, participate in money laundering, or manipulate the transaction systems.

## 7. Service Availability (Offline First)
SCS utilizes an Offline-First approach. Downloaded courses and library assets remain accessible during internet outages. However, real-time communication, AI interactions, and new transaction processing require an active internet connection.

## 8. Amendments
SCS reserves the right to update these Terms of Service at any time. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
