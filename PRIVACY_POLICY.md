# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: April 2026**

At **SCS (Shazly Community for Students)**, we prioritize your privacy while providing cutting-edge educational and social tools. This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** Name, email, role, avatar, and bio.
- **Educational Data:** Course progress, test scores, read tracking, and downloaded resources.
- **Financial Information:** Managed securely by third-party providers (e.g., PayPal, Fawry). We strictly store transaction histories, subscription statuses, and wallet balances, never raw card details.
- **Device & Usage Data:** IP addresses, device types (for performance optimization), and interaction metrics.

## 2. Communications and Chat (E2EE)
- **Direct Messaging:** Direct, 1-on-1 private messages utilize End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) where supported. SCS cannot read the content of these messages.
- **Group/Community Chat:** Data is encrypted at rest but is accessible to moderation systems to enforce community guidelines.
- **Ephemeral Content:** "Stories" and self-destructing messages are deleted from our servers once their expiration time is reached.

## 3. Smart Moderation & Reporting
SCS employs an AI Smart Moderation system to maintain a respectful environment.
If a user is officially reported for a violation (e.g., harassment, sensitive content):
- The AI algorithm compiles a chat audit.
- **Note:** To prevent abuse, even messages that a user attempts to "delete" might be logged temporarily to facilitate accurate reporting audits before permanent erasure.

## 4. Screenshot Detection
To protect the intellectual property (IP) of Creators and Teachers, SCS employs browser-based tools to detect and block screen recording and screenshots.
- If a screenshot attempt is detected during private conversations, the opposing party may be notified automatically depending on privacy configurations.

## 5. Storage and Third-Party Services
Files and videos uploaded to SCS are processed and stored using decentralized object storage (e.g., Cloudflare R2).
- **Video Metadata:** Processed via FFmpeg workers to ensure fast, scalable streaming (HLS).
- **AI Tooling:** Requests made to the integrated `@SCS_AI` or internal productivity suites (Docs, Sheets) may be processed by third-party proprietary LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI or DeepSeek backend) strictly for request fulfillment. User data is not used to train these external models.

## 6. Offline-First Capabilities (Local Storage)
For seamless study, SCS syncs necessary data (like purchased courses or library books) directly to your device’s local indexed storage. This data remains on your machine and is synchronized automatically with our cloud when your connection is restored.

## 7. Your Rights
Under applicable data privacy laws, you have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data.
- Request deletion of your account and associated social profile (some financial records must be retained by law).
- Modify or update your Profile.
- Control visibility of your ephemeral stories and profile details via Privacy Settings.

For data requests, please contact the SCS Support team via the Admin Dashboard or standard support email.
